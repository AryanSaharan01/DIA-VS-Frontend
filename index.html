DIA Club

The DIA (Design, Interior, Architecture) Club is a creative hub for students passionate about spatial aesthetics, architectural innovation, and interior design. It provides a platform for aspiring designers to explore the intersection of art, functionality, and structural planning.

Beyond technical learning, the DIA Club promotes sustainability and smart design by encouraging the use of eco-friendly materials, energy-efficient structures, and innovative urban planning concepts.

Frequently Asked Questions

You can join the DIA Club by registering through our website or visiting our club office during the registration period at the beginning of each semester.

No prior experience is required! We welcome students from all backgrounds who are interested in design, architecture, and interior spaces.

We organize workshops, guest lectures, design competitions, site visits, exhibitions, and collaborative projects throughout the year.

There is a nominal membership fee of $20 per semester which helps cover materials and event costs. Scholarships are available for those in need.
